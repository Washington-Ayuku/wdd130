History

Centuries ago, Native Americans crafted very sturdy and woodenly rafts to access the waters on the huntingup for the rich fishing spots. The raft building skills learned and passed through generations of Native Americans were eventually passed on to Lewis and Clarkas they made their journey west through the greatness Northwest.
